The origin

We built the tool
we desperately
needed ourselves

"I was billing $90 an hour and losing track of three hours a week. That's $14,000 a year I just didn't invoice for. The tools I was using weren't talking to each other."
— Alex Kovacs, Co-founder, Clientli

In 2022, Alex Kovacs was a freelance product designer earning a good living in Berlin. On paper, the business was working. In practice, it was hemorrhaging money in ways that were nearly invisible.

Three hours of unbilled work per week. An average invoice payment time of 52 days. A proposal win rate of 28%. Seven tools — Toggl, Wave, Notion, Calendly, DocuSign, Stripe, Gmail — each doing one thing, none of them aware of the others.

The frustrating part wasn't any single tool. It was the space between them. The manual data entry. The context switching. The time spent reconciling information across systems that should have been connected from the start.

Alex built the first version of Clientli for personal use in early 2023. Within three months of using it, he'd increased his monthly invoiced revenue by 31% — not by working more, but by capturing what he was already doing.

He showed it to two other freelancers. They asked if they could use it. He showed it to ten more. Same response. By mid-2023, Clientli was a company.

What we believe

The principles that
shape everything we build

🔗
Connection over collection
Separate tools that don't talk to each other aren't a workflow — they're a liability. Every feature in Clientli exists inside a connected system, not as a standalone module bolted on for feature parity.
📊
Data belongs to the freelancer
Your client list, your invoice history, your time logs — they're yours. We don't hold your data hostage behind a cancellation wall. Export everything, anytime, in open formats.
🚫
No percentage. Ever.
We charge a flat subscription. We never take a cut of your invoices. The moment we tie our revenue to your invoice volume, our incentives stop being aligned with yours. That's a line we won't cross.
⚡
Speed is a feature
Creating an invoice should take 30 seconds, not 8 minutes. If a workflow feels slow in Clientli, that's a bug — not an expected UX. We obsess over the time cost of every interaction.
🌍
Built for independence
Freelancing is a choice that deserves to be supported, not patronised. We build tools that respect your intelligence, your autonomy, and your right to run your business however you see fit.
🔒
Privacy by design
Your client data, your revenue, your business performance — these are sensitive. We don't monetise your data. We don't sell aggregated insights about your business. We just build good software.
Our mission
"Every independent professional deserves business infrastructure as good as any well-funded company — without the overhead of building it themselves."

Clientli exists to close the gap between the experience of running a business as an employee of a well-resourced company, and the reality of doing it alone. The tools exist. The integration has always been the missing piece.
