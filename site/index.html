How AI Clientli works

Set up in minutes.
Run your business in one place.

1
Add your clients and active projects
Import from CSV or add manually. AI Clientli creates a full profile for each client — linking their projects, invoices, contracts, and communication history in one view from day one.
2
Track time and assign it to projects
Start a timer from any project view. AI Clientli logs every session, marks it as billable or non-billable, and accumulates hours automatically — ready to pull directly into an invoice.
3
Send invoices built from your tracked time
One click generates a polished invoice pre-populated with tracked hours, project milestones, and your payment terms. Send via Stripe, PayPal, or bank transfer — and let auto-reminders handle the rest.
4
Watch your analytics update in real time
Revenue charts, profitability breakdowns, client lifetime value, and time utilisation — all updating live as payments arrive and projects progress. Know your business at a glance every single morning.

Common questions

Everything you want to know
before you start

FreshBooks and Wave are primarily accounting tools. Harvest is a time tracker. None of them treat the full freelance workflow — from proposal to contract to time-tracking to invoicing to analytics — as a connected system. AI Clientli was designed from the ground up for independent professionals who need a single command center, not another accounting add-on. You also never pay per-invoice or per-seat fees on Pro.

Yes. AI Clientli supports CSV import for clients, projects, and invoice history. If you're migrating from FreshBooks, Wave, Harvest, or Toggl, we have dedicated import guides that walk you through the process in under 20 minutes. Your historical data stays yours — you can also export everything at any time.

Never. You pay a flat monthly or annual subscription — AI Clientli does not take a cut of your earnings at any tier. Payment processing fees (charged by Stripe or PayPal) are the only transaction-level costs, and those go directly to the payment processor, not to us.

When you close out a billing period, AI Clientli pulls all logged billable hours for a client, applies your hourly rate (or project-level overrides), and pre-populates a ready-to-send invoice. You review, adjust if needed, and send — no manual data entry, no spreadsheet in between. Time entries can also be attached as line-item detail for full transparency with clients.

AI Clientli's desktop app runs on Linux, macOS, and Windows. A companion mobile app for iOS and Android is in active development and expected in Q2 2025 — early access is available to Pro subscribers. The web version is fully mobile-responsive and works on any device browser today.

Your data belongs to you. If you cancel, you have 30 days to export everything — clients, projects, invoices, contracts, time entries — in CSV or PDF format. We don't delete your data immediately; you'll receive export reminders before any data is removed. We also offer a read-only archive mode for 12 months post-cancellation on request.
